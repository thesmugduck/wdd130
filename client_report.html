Reflections

I think the most challenging part of this project was that we were all thrown together into 4 different groups at the same time. And it was hard to keep track of everyone and what I was supposed to be doing. The communication was terrible. If I could go back and do things differently I would set up group chats for every team and use phone numbers instead of email. I did enjoy seeing all of the projects come to life and my site being made. I feel a lot more comfortable with working on a real life senario now because I know how to improve and I know a lot more about GitHub.
